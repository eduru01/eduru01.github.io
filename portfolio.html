Payday Lender Location and Income Analysis

Investigated the relationship between payday loan lender locations and household income levels in Houston, Texas using spatial analysis. Mapped payday lender locations and analyzed their distribution relative to traditional bank branches and median household income across census tracts and zip codes. Utilized ArcGIS Pro with spatial join, Local Moran's I clustering analysis, and attribute queries to identify patterns of financial exclusion and visualize structural inequality in financial institution access.
